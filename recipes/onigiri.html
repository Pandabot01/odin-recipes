Onigiri

Description

Onigiri are Japanese rice balls, they make perfect snacks and are quick to make.
Cook time is less than 10 minutes, longest part is cooking rice.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Salt
  • Seaweed
  • Furikake

Steps

  1. Cook rice.
  2. Mix rice with salt and furikake.
  3. Make ball and wrap with seaweed.
