Oyakodon

Description

Oyakodon is a Japanese dish made of egg, chicken, and onion simmered and served over rice.
Cook time is less than 30 minutes, and oyakodon is perfect for breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 2 Chicken thighs (boneless and skinless)
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1/2 Onion
  • 1 tbsp Soy Sauce
  • 1 tbsp Sugar
  • 1 tbsp Sake
  • 1 tbsp Mirin
  • 1/2 cup Dashi
  • 2 cups Rice
  • Extra: Green onions

Steps

  1. Start your rice, as it probably is going to take the longest.
  2. Bring your dashi to a boil in a small pan over medium heat.
  3. Add your onions and cook until browned.
  4. Add Chicken thighs, soy sauce, mirin, sake, and sugar to the small pan cook for 3-5 minutes.
  5. Whisk your eggs in and add to pan once chicken is ready.
  6. Once egg and chicken is cooked serve over a bowl of rice and add green onions if you want.
  7. Enjoy.
