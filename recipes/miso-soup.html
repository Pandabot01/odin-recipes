Miso Soup

Description

Miso soup is a very traditional Japanese soup made of dashi and miso, usually with a little seaweed and tofu.
First you will either need to make your own dashi, or use dashi powder/paste.

Ingredients

  • 200 ml Dashi Powder/ Or make your own.
  • 1 tbsp Miso paste
  • Seaweed
  • Tofu
  • Green Onion

Steps

  1. Heat Dashi powder and water medium heat.
  2. Add miso paste and stir till dissolved.
  3. Add tofu, seaweed and green onion.
  4. Enjoy.
