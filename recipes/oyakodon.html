Oyakodon

Description

Oyakodon is a Japanese dish made of egg and chicken, and served over rice.
Cook time is less than 30 minutes, and oyakodon is perfect for breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 2 Chicken thighs (boneless and skinless)
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1/2 Onion
  • 1 & 1/2 Tbsp Soy Sauce
  • 1 & 1/2 Tbsp Sugar
  • 1 & 1/2 Tbsp Sake
  • 1 & 1/2 Mirin
  • 2 Cups Rice

Steps

  1. Cook onion and chicken and add soy sauce, mirin, sake and sugar
